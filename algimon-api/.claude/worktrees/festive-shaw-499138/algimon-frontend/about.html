About Us

Our History and Commitment

From humble beginnings to a trusted name in fire protection and auxiliary systems.

Algimon Fire Protection Services (AFPS), formerly Altheoc's Trading, is a trusted provider of Fire Protection and Auxiliary Systems since 2008. Established as a sole proprietorship in 2020, AFPS specializes in the supply, installation, and maintenance of Automatic Sprinkler and Fire Alarm Detection Systems, serving well-known clients across various industries.
